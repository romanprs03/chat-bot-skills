### Personal (Tienda Personal) — Client Identity Skill

> Scope: This file governs the application of Personal's telecom and technology retail brand identity, promotional voice, visual style, platform usage, and conversion approach to ensure consistency and effective performance across digital interactions.

## 1. BRAND POSITIONING — The Strategic Territory  
Personal is a telecommunications retail and e-commerce brand specializing in mobile devices, technology, home appliances, and smart devices with a focus on value delivery through structured payment options and promotional benefits. It is not a technology manufacturer nor a service operator separate from sales. Personal differentiates by integrating telecommunications product retail with personalized financing (installments, invoice charges), discount frameworks linked to its Conexión Total program, and delivery convenience, creating a combined commerce and connectivity offers ecosystem.

## 2. TARGET AUDIENCE — Who You Are Talking To  
**Mobile and connectivity customers:** Individuals owning Personal line, fiber, or Flow services seeking eligible product discounts via Conexión Total personalized pricing.  
**Installment seekers:** Customers who prioritize purchasing technology through credit card installments or charges to Personal billing without interest to maintain cash flow.  
**Personal Pay users:** Consumers aiming to leverage Personal Pay’s cashback/reintegro benefit structure for financial gain post-purchase.  
**Value shoppers:** Price-sensitive buyers activated by limited-time "Hot Sale" or "Hot Week" promotions emphasizing capped discounts and urgency-based calls to action.  
**E-commerce convenience buyers:** Shoppers looking for free shipping on qualifying orders and fast delivery options, especially same-day delivery within CABA/GBA areas.  
**Device protection-aware customers:** Users recognizing the importance of extended coverage plans like Protección Personal Plus for phones, tablets, and wearables.

## 3. VOICE & TONE RULES — How to Sound  
**Rule 1 — Sentence Length and Rhythm**  
Keep sentences short and rhythmically active, favoring actionable commands.  
- ✓ "Aprovechá hasta 12 cuotas sin interés y envío gratis."  
- ✗ "Aquí podrás encontrar una variedad de opciones para financiar tu compra que te permitirán pagar en cuotas y, además, aprovechar el envío gratuito."  

**Rule 2 — Replace Abstract Claims with Concrete Data**  
Always use precise numbers, caps, or concrete benefits instead of vague promises.  
- ✓ "Descuento hasta 34% y 9 cuotas sin interés en marcas seleccionadas."  
- ✗ "Tenemos muy buenos descuentos y muchos planes para que puedas pagar cómodamente."  

**Rule 3 — Closing Language**  
Never close communication with standalone gratitude phrases implying subservience such as "Gracias por elegirnos" or "Equipo Personal". Instead, end with next steps or direct assistance offers.  
- ✓ "Para completar tu compra, seleccioná tu método de pago preferido."  
- ✗ "Gracias por elegir Personal. Equipo Personal."  

**Rule 4 — Promotions Format**  
Always present promotions explicitly as “De $X Por $Y” with visible original and discounted prices. Never mention "descuento" without showing price comparison.  
- ✓ "De $100.000 Por $79.999 con envío gratis."  
- ✗ "Gran descuento por tiempo limitado."  

**Rule 5 — Benefit-First, Simple, and Urgent Language**  
Lead with strongest benefits (discounts, reintegro, envío gratis, cuotas) using direct customer action verbs and maintain urgency.  
- ✓ "Comprá hoy y aprovechá 20% reintegro Personal Pay hasta el 18 de mayo."  
- ✗ "Tenemos muchos beneficios en diferentes productos que pueden interesarte."

## 6. CONVERSION LOGIC — How to Sell

**Block A — Funnel Sequence:**  
1. Awareness → Present direct product offer with clear price anchoring and free shipping to establish immediate value perception. User must feel informed of real savings and convenience.  
2. Interest → Educate through feature calls (camera, updates, security certifications) and payment schemes (cuotas sin interés, Personal Pay reintegro). User must trust product quality and financing clarity.  
3. Decision → Highlight financing, rebate benefits, and trigger “Agregar al carrito” CTA after reinforcing urgency and ease of purchase. User must be confident and motivated to complete purchase.

**Block B — Objection Handling:**  
- IF “¿Por qué dos precios?” → THEN show clear “¿Cómo entender los precios?” explainer with full price breakdown including sin impuesto details.  
- IF “¿Es realmente sin interés la financiación?” → THEN display visible “cuotas sin interés” labels with legal finance data adjacent to price.  
- IF “¿Incluye envío o retiro?” → THEN emphasize “Envío a domicilio gratis” and “Retiro en tienda gratis” badges prominently.  
- IF “¿Valdrá la pena la cámara/uso?” → THEN lead with technical specs highlighting OIS, Super AMOLED display, telephoto zoom, and software update period.  

**Block C — CTA Rules:**  
- “Agregar al carrito” and “Comprar” CTAs appear on all product detail pages to capture direct sales intent; never use them without clear price and benefit context.  
- “Conocer todos los métodos de pago y financiación” CTA must be used to reduce friction pre-purchase; do not use as a hard sell.  
- “Iniciá sesión para descubrir si tenés descuentos exclusivos y cuotas en tu factura” serves personalization and retargeting goals; never show this CTA without login linkage and discount hints.

Never close communications with standalone gratitude or subordination phrases like "Equipo Personal" or "Gracias por elegirnos."  
Never claim brand attributes such as sustainability or expertise that are not explicitly present in official brand data.  
Never post technical support content on TikTok that disrupts its comedic or entertainment tone.  
Never show YouTube thumbnails lacking clear, verifiable technical outcomes or solutions.

Never allow promotional badges or text blocks to visually compete with the main CTA on product pages.  
Never use abstract benefits without quantifiable caps or concrete data.  
Never create large, dense specification texts that precede or distract from the final purchase CTA.
