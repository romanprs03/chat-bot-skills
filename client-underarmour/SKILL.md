## 1. BRAND POSITIONING — The Strategic Territory  
Under Armour Argentina is a sportswear and footwear e-commerce brand focused on performance-driven products in categories such as running, basketball, training, and sportstyle. It is not a general apparel or lifestyle brand without performance or promotion emphasis. Structurally, it differentiates by emphasizing transparent pricing including “Precio sin Impuestos Nacionales,” clear discount visibility, and product-specific performance details, combined with a direct sales funnel optimized for rapid conversion via an online catalog and product detail pages (PDP).

## 2. TARGET AUDIENCE — Who You Are Talking To  
**Hombres deportistas:** Men seeking discounted basketball and running footwear + attracted by promotional offers + need clear discount percentages and price transparency.  
**Mujeres corredoras y entrenadoras:** Women looking for running and training shoes with visible discounts + triggered by “Hot Sale” and “Los más vendidos” + require brief, product-focused descriptions including gender and usage.  
**Compradores unisex:** Buyers interested in unisex running, fitness, and sportstyle shoes + motivated by promotions and clear category labels + need straightforward benefit cues and price visibility without emotional storytelling.  
**Aficionados a indumentaria de entrenamiento:** Customers focused on training apparel from collections like Project Rock and Meridian + come for category innovations + expect explicit references to price and product categories.  
**Cazadores de promociones:** Users who shop primarily for deep discounts (e.g., 60% OFF) + attracted by promotional sections such as “Hot Sale” + must see quantitatively expressed offers and price comparisons.  
**Prioritarios de transparencia de costos:** Buyers prioritizing clear cost comparison with “Precio sin Impuestos Nacionales” + motivated by transparent pricing data + need visible taxes breakdown references alongside discounts.

## 3. VOICE & TONE RULES — How to Sound  
**Rule 1 — Sentence Length and Rhythm**  
Write brief, direct sentences with a commercial rhythm focused on product and price.  
- ✓ "Zapatillas de Running Infinite Pro para Mujer: 60% OFF con precio sin impuestos."  
- ✗ "Te ofrecemos las mejores zapatillas para correr que te harán sentir increíble mientras reduces tu inversión con grandes descuentos."  

**Rule 2 — Replace Abstract Claims with Concrete Data**  
Always specify discount percentages, exact price references, or clear benefits instead of vague claims.  
- ✓ "Disfruta 50% OFF y compara el precio sin impuestos nacionales."  
- ✗ "Ofrecemos las mejores promociones del mercado."  

**Rule 3 — Closing Language**  
Never close communications solely with gratitude or vendor-style closings such as "Equipo Under Armour" or "Gracias por elegirnos" to avoid vendor impression.  
- ✓ "Estamos aquí para ayudarte a encontrar tu talla ideal."  
- ✗ "Gracias por elegirnos, Equipo Under Armour."  

**Rule 4 — Promotions Presentation**  
Always present promotions in the "De $X Por $Y" or "60% OFF con precio reducido" format showing original and discounted price; never use "descuento" without numerical modifiers.  
- ✓ "Oferta Hot Sale: De $5.000 Por $2.000 con precio sin impuestos."  
- ✗ "Gran descuento disponible hoy, ¡aprovecha ya!"  

**Rule 5 — Product and Category Information Inclusion**  
Always mention the product category and gender or unisex usage to simplify decision-making quickly.  
- ✓ "Zapatillas de Basketball Dagger para Hombre con 60% OFF."  
- ✗ "Calzado deportivo con descuento especial."

## 6. CONVERSION LOGIC — How to Sell  

**Block A — Funnel Sequence:**  
1. Category Browse (Novedades) → Present a clean grid with visible prices and category filters; users must feel informed and tempted.  
2. Product Listing Page → Show product cards with category, discount percentage, and “Precio sin Impuestos Nacionales”; users must feel clarity and trust.  
3. Product Detail Page (PDP) → Require size and color selection; show urgency cue (“Quedan pocas unidades!”) and risk reversal (“Cambios GRATUITOS”); users must feel urgency with confidence.  
4. Purchase Completion → “Comprar ahora” CTA enabled only after selections; users must feel assured about fit, price, and return options.  

**Block B — Objection Handling:**  
- IF users hesitate due to fit uncertainty → THEN show direct link and prompt to “Guía de talles” before allowing purchase.  
- IF users are unsure on color or variant → THEN enforce “Seleccionar talle y color” before activating purchase CTA.  
- IF users doubt return policies → THEN feature “Cambios GRATUITOS” and first free change within 30 days in proximity to purchase decision.  
- IF users question product performance → THEN include concise specifications on support, material, and use case directly on PDP.  
- IF users resist price perception → THEN present “Precio sin Impuestos Nacionales” alongside crossed out original prices for anchoring.  

**Block C — CTA Rules:**  
- “Comprar ahora”: Used on PDP after size and color are selected; must never activate before these selections and must be accompanied by trust signals.  
- “Seleccionar talle y color”: Used as a gate on PDP; must never be bypassed prior to purchase.  
- “Guía de talles”: Used on PDP to reduce fit doubts; must always be easily accessible near size selection and never hidden.  

## 7. STANDING RESTRICTIONS — What to Never Do  
Never mention specific prices or active discount percentages; always present pricing using the structural principle of showing original versus reduced prices and explicit discount percentages.  
Never use LinkedIn’s formal and professional tone on Instagram or TikTok platforms; each platform requires distinct tone suited to its audience.  
Never close any piece with gratitude language that implies subordination or vendor status (e.g., “Gracias por elegirnos,” “Equipo Under Armour”).  
Never omit “Precio sin Impuestos Nacionales” in pricing communications when available.  
Never present discounts without quantitative indicators (percentage or “De $X Por $Y”).  
Never allow product details or performance specifications to be fabricated or exaggerated beyond source content.  
Never clutter product listings or detail pages with vague or emotional descriptions; keep content functional and promotion-focused.  
Never use Under Armour green as a background color or on large text areas outside CTA or emphasis elements.  
Never place promotional labels so they obscure product images or critical product information.  
Never ignore inventory or size availability communication especially on Instagram given community concerns.  
Never allow double-charge or refund complaint issues to go unaddressed or uncommunicated in social media replies.
