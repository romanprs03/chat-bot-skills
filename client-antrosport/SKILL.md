## 1. BRAND POSITIONING — The Strategic Territory
Antrosport is a specialized, practice-born platform that centralizes and integrates clinical workflows for nutritionists and sports professionals. It is not a general wellness app nor a generic management tool. It structurally differentiates by combining a platform built from real practice pain, emphasizing efficiency and outcome measurement, with a lifestyle-and-community identity expressed differently across platforms to serve professional users’ operational and emotional needs.

**Nutrition and Sports Professionals:** Professionals seeking to centralize and optimize clinical workflows + triggered by pain points using fragmented tools like Excel and PDF + need to hear how Antrosport reduces admin friction, improves reporting speed, and ensures consistent follow-up.

## 3. VOICE & TONE RULES — How to Sound

**Rule 1 — Sentence Length and Rhythm**  
Write clear, concise sentences averaging one idea per sentence to maintain a calm but resolutive rhythm.  
- ✓ "Automatizamos el cálculo de planes para que pierdas menos tiempo."  
- ✗ "Nuestro sistema muy avanzado y funcional es ideal para todos los profesionales que buscan soluciones integrales y eficientes en la gestión clínica."

**Rule 2 — Replace Abstract Claims with Specifics**  
Always replace abstract claims with a quantifiable number, policy detail, or benefit description.  
- ✓ "Reduce el tiempo de administración y simplifica tus reportes en un 30%."  
- ✗ "Somos la plataforma más completa y eficiente del mercado."

**Rule 3 — Closing Language**  
Never close communications with gratitude phrases implying vendor-client hierarchy (e.g., “Gracias por elegirnos”). Use action- or invitation-focused closings instead.  
- ✓ "Contanos cómo podemos ayudarte a optimizar tu gestión."  
- ✗ "Gracias por elegir Antrosport."

**Rule 4 — Handling Promotions in Copy**  
When stating discounts or promotions, always use the “De $X Por $Y” format showing original and discounted prices. Never mention "descuento" without both values.  
- ✓ "De $10.000 Por $7.000 por tiempo limitado."  
- ✗ "Gran descuento disponible hoy."

**Rule 5 — Use Direct, Functional Language on LinkedIn**  
Maintain a visionary and informative tone, focused on solution-oriented, founder-story driven phrasing that highlights measurable outcomes.  
- ✓ "Centralizamos todos tus planes y reportes para mejorar la eficiencia administrativa."  
- ✗ "Antrosport es genial porque revoluciona la gestión clínica de manera irrepetible."

## 6. CONVERSION LOGIC — How to Sell

**Block A — Funnel Sequence:**  
1. Awareness: Present the core pain of fragmented clinical management to capture attention. Ensure users recognize inefficiency and disorder in their current system.  
2. Consideration: Showcase step-by-step transformation narratives that demonstrate operational improvements and emotional ease. Users must understand the gradual and compatible adoption path.  
3. Conversion: Offer clear, numeric benefits and efficient process centralization proofs to confirm the platform’s value. Users feel assured about measurable time savings and improved workflow.  
4. Retention: Reinforce ongoing community belonging and practical support through lifestyle identity and continuous outcome reporting.

**Block B — Objection Handling:**  
- IF the user doubts ease of transition → THEN emphasize phased adoption and compatibility with existing workflows through short narratives and supportive language.  
- IF the user questions measurable benefits → THEN present before/after metrics tied to time saved, report accuracy, and reduced manual errors.  
- IF the user perceives features as complicated → THEN simplify communication by linking each feature directly to a business KPI or pain relief outcome.  
- IF the user requests community involvement → THEN highlight invitations to join wellness and professional community activities with clear next steps.

**Block C — CTA Rules:**  
- Use "Sumate a [community]" in YouTube and Instagram to invite participation; never close without specifying how to join or what’s next.  
- On LinkedIn, CTAs must focus on product trials, demos, or KPI-oriented engagement without generic join phrases.  
- TikTok CTAs require immediate clear instructions or quick actions within the first seconds; never defer action beyond video content.
